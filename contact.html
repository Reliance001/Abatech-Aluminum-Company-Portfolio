Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does a typical installation take?

+

Most residential window or door installations can be completed in 1-3 days. Larger projects or roofing installations may take 1-2 weeks depending on scope.

Do you offer free consultations?

+

Yes! We provide complimentary on-site consultations where we assess your needs, take measurements, and provide a detailed quote.

What is your warranty policy?

+

We offer a comprehensive 10-year warranty on installations and up to 25 years on materials, depending on the product.

Can you match existing designs?

+

Absolutely! We specialize in custom fabrication and can match or complement your existing architectural style.

Do you handle commercial projects?

+

Yes, we work with both residential and commercial clients, handling projects of all sizes.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept cash, bank transfers, all major credit cards, and offer financing options for qualified customers.
